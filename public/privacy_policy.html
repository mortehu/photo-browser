Privacy Policy for Album Viewer

Album Viewer is a web application that allows users to view their Google Photos albums in a simpler user interface. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle and protect your personal information when using Album Viewer.

Information Collection and Use

Album Viewer does not collect or store any personal information from its users. All data received from Google Photos, including authentication tokens, is stored only in the user's web browser and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Third-Party Services

Album Viewer uses Google Photos API to access your Google Photos album data. By using Album Viewer, you agree to Google Photos API's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. You can review Google Photos API's Privacy Policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Security

The security of your personal information is important to us. Album Viewer runs entirely in your web browser, and no data is transmitted to or stored on our servers. We use Google infrastructure to serve the application, ensuring high levels of security and reliability.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us.
